Our story

Inchoo was founded in 2008 Osijek, Croatia as a full service eCommerce development agency. Our purpose is to help merchants start and grow their online businesses.

We strive to be a merchant’s best friend! Each merchant has a unique story and so is our approach. By combining consulting, development and design services aligned with the culture based on integrity, excellence and passion, we are able to prepare and execute a high-performing commerce strategy.

We have experience with various eCommerce platforms, but we are mostly recognized as Magento experts. Our clients operate in different industries and niches, and we carefully help them select the right solution and product bundle for their business.

Our work doesn’t stop with the launch of the website. We keep our clients close and strive to establish long-term relationships by helping them to achieve their goals in every business aspect.

We got you covered across the board, with technical stability, website security, feature innovation, and overall marketing strategy across different channels like search engines, PPC, social media, email and many more.

If you need to have your existing website migrated to Magento 2, we’re here for you. If you need a fresh new online store, we’re here for you. And if you want to achieve better results with your current store, guess what? You got it – we’re here for you!

All you need to do is fill out our contact form and we’ll take it from there!
